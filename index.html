Welcome to the Institute for Research & Policy Alternatives

A full-service boutique consulting firm to meet the needs of the public and private sectors in Kenya and throughout East Africa, the Institute for Research & Policy Alternatives (IRPA) is registered in Kenya as a private limited company since 2012.
